Crispy Fried Chicken

Overview

This crispy fried chicken recipe delivers crunchy skin and juicy meat, perfect for a delicious meal.

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (thighs, drumsticks, or wings) - 1 kg
  • All-Purpose Flour - 2 cups
  • Garlic Powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Chili sauce - to taste
  • Dried Oregano - to taste
  • Ground ginger - to taste
  • Chili powder - to taste
  • Anise - to taste
  • Egg - 1/2 whole
  • Water - 1/4 cup
  • Baking powder - 1 tsp
  • Cooking oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Season the 1 kg chicken pieces in a bowl with garlic powder, salt, ground black pepper, and chili sauce. You can also add dried oregano, ground ginger, chili powder, and anise, according to your taste.
  2. Prepare the coating: Combine flour, egg, and water in a separate bowl. Mix until smooth and slightly thick.
  3. Coat the chicken: Dip every piece of chicken into the flour mixture to ensure full coverage for easy frying.
  4. Dip the chicken in eggs: Dip all chicken pieces in a mixture of flour, egg, water, and milk. This creates a thick coating on the chicken pieces that lock in the natural flavor and moisture of the meat.
  5. Fry the chicken pieces: Fry the coated chicken completely in the oil. Fry one time for 6 to 10 minutes, then turn and fry the other side for another 6 to 10 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Rest and enjoy: Transfer the chicken to a wire rack to drain excess oil. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving with gravy or other sauces and rice.
