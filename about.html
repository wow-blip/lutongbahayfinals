About Us

Lutong Bahay is a platform dedicated to celebrating and sharing the rich tapestry of Filipino cuisines. We believe that food is more than just sustenance—it's a reflection of our culture, traditions, and the love we pour into every dish. Our website showcases authentic recipes from various regions of the Philippines, honoring the influences of Native traditions as well as Spanish, Chinese, and American cuisines that have shaped our culinary heritage.

Whether you're a seasoned cook or just starting your journey into Filipino food, Lutong Bahay is your kitchen companion. Join us in exploring, learning, and celebrating the flavors that bring our communities together.
