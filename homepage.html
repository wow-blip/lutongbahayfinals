Recipe of the Week

Sisig

By Anna Marie

Sisig is a famous Filipino dish made from chopped pork—usually pig’s face and ears—seasoned with calamansi, onions, and chili peppers. Originating from Pampanga, it’s known for its sizzling, savory flavor and crispy texture. Sisig is often served on a hot plate and enjoyed as a main dish or pulutan (bar chow), representing the creativity and bold taste of Filipino cuisine
