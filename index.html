Spanish influenced

Spanish-influenced cuisine in the Philippines showcases dishes introduced during the Spanish colonial period, blending local ingredients with Spanish flavors and cooking techniques. Popular examples include adobo, paella, menudo, and lechon, which highlight rich sauces, savory spices, and festive presentation. These dishes reflect the lasting impact of Spanish culture on Filipino food traditions.
